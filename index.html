About This Course

This blended learning course is designed for Form 2 students in rural Tanzania who have limited access to computers and the internet. Our approach combines:

  • 30% in-school computer lab sessions
  • 70% mobile-accessible online content
  • Interactive tutorials and quizzes
  • Peer collaboration opportunities
  • Low-bandwidth optimized materials
